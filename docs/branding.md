# Branding

## Project Name

**Bloodlines**

## Taglines

Primary:

> Evolution begins with survival.

Secondary:

> A genetic progression overhaul for 7 Days to Die.

## Visual Direction

Bloodlines uses a gritty rediscovered-biotechnology style.

The world should feel like survivors are rebuilding abandoned genetic research from scavenged parts, not using clean futuristic technology.

## Style Notes

Use:
- ruined laboratories
- cracked specimen vials
- damaged DNA samples
- improvised sequencing equipment
- industrial debris
- flickering monitors
- military research cases
- post-apocalyptic lighting

Avoid:
- clean sci-fi holograms
- superhero visuals
- neon cyberpunk excess
- overly polished lab equipment
