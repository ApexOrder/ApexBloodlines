# Bloodlines

Bloodlines are the personal evolution layer of the mod.

They belong to the player, not the base.

## Core Rule

> If a feature makes the player stronger, it belongs to the DNA system.

Bloodlines represent controlled genetic adaptation after the collapse.

## Personal Progression

```text
XP
↓
Attributes
↓
Perks
↓
Bloodline
↓
DNA Helixes
↓
Genetic Stability
```

## DNA Helixes

DNA Helixes are personal progression items.

When integrated, they permanently alter the survivor.

They are not stored in the Keystone.

## Genetic Stability

Genetic Stability represents how safely a survivor can integrate advanced DNA.

Higher stability allows deeper Bloodline progression and access to more advanced DNA Locks.

## Six Starting Bloodlines

Version 1.0 is planned around six core Bloodlines:

- TERRA — mining, extraction, geology, endurance
- FORGE — crafting, manufacturing, repairs, equipment
- MACHINA — engineering, electricity, vehicles, machinery
- GAIA — farming, hunting, food, water, medicine
- ORION — exploration, scouting, lockpicking, treasure
- AEGIS — combat, defence, resilience, horde survival

## Design Rule

Bloodlines should change how a player approaches the world, not simply add bigger numbers.
