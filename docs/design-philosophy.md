# Design Philosophy

Bloodlines is guided by a strict design constitution.

## Design Lock #001

> Characters evolve through DNA.
>
> Bases evolve through knowledge.

This rule is permanent unless changed through an approved RFC.

## Core Principles

### Discover, Don't Explain

Players should uncover systems naturally through exploration, items, POIs, and environmental storytelling.

### Evolution Through Survival

Progression should feel like controlled biological adaptation, not simple levelling.

### Technology Is Rediscovered

Technology should feel recovered, repaired, and rebuilt from abandoned laboratories, industrial equipment, military surplus, and scavenged machinery.

### Exploration Must Always Matter

Every expedition should have the chance to advance the player, the base, or the story.

### Solo Is Always Viable

Nothing should require a group.

A solo player may progress more slowly, but should never be locked out of core content.

### Cooperation Accelerates Progression

Multiplayer should reward specialisation and cooperation without making every player identical.

### Knowledge Unlocks Technology

Research Notes and Research Archives advance the base.

### DNA Unlocks Capability

DNA Fragments, DNA Helixes, and Genetic Stability evolve the survivor.

## Final Rule

If a feature does not reinforce the fantasy of rebuilding humanity through rediscovered biotechnology, it probably does not belong in Bloodlines.
