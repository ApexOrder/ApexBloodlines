# DNA Helixes

DNA Helixes replace the earlier Tome concept.

They are physical progression items created through genetic sequencing and used to advance Bloodlines and Keystone technology.

## Core Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| DNA Fragment | Raw unstable genetic material recovered from the world |
| Damaged DNA Sample | A more valuable sample requiring analysis |
| Purified DNA | Stabilised genetic material ready for sequencing |
| DNA Helix | A usable genetic progression item |
| Apex Helix | A late-game/master-level Helix |
| Genetic Stability | The survivor's ability to safely integrate advanced genetic progression |
| Helix Slot | A Keystone slot that accepts a DNA Helix |
| DNA Sequencing | The process of upgrading or creating Helixes |

## Progression Concept

```text
Zombie DNA
↓
Damaged DNA Sample
↓
Purified DNA
↓
DNA Helix α
↓
DNA Helix β
↓
DNA Helix γ
↓
Apex Helix
```

## Design Rule

DNA systems should feel like rediscovered biotechnology, not clean futuristic science fiction.
