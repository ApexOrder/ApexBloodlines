# DNA Locks

DNA Locks are one of the core systems that make Bloodlines different from a normal perk overhaul.

## Core Rule

> Knowledge tells you how. DNA allows you to do it.

In vanilla progression, a player usually unlocks technology through level, perks, schematics, or magazines.

In Bloodlines, advanced technology can require both knowledge and genetic capability.

## Why DNA Locks Exist

Vanilla perks can become powerful quickly. If they remain the only gate, Bloodlines becomes optional.

DNA Locks ensure that major progression is tied to evolution, exploration, and genetic sequencing.

## Example

```text
Steel Pickaxe

Requirements:
✔ Mining Knowledge
✔ Forging Research
✔ Workstation Access
❌ TERRA Helix α
```

The player may understand how to make the item, but cannot produce or fully use it until their survivor has evolved enough.

## What DNA Locks Can Gate

DNA Locks may be used for:

- advanced tools
- specialist weapons
- high-tier armour
- industrial workstations
- advanced vehicles
- specialised crafting recipes
- Bloodline-specific technologies

## What DNA Locks Should Not Do

DNA Locks should not make early survival impossible.

Basic survival, primitive crafting, and emergency tools should remain available to every player.

## Design Rule

Perks improve efficiency.

Bloodlines unlock capability.
