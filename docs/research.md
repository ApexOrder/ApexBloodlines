# Research

Research is the base progression layer of Bloodlines.

## Core Rule

> Characters evolve through DNA. Bases evolve through knowledge.

Research does not make the player biologically stronger. It advances the base, unlocks infrastructure, and restores lost technology.

## Research Items

### Research Notes

Research Notes are fragments of recovered knowledge found through exploration.

They may come from:

- laboratories
- hospitals
- factories
- military sites
- trader rewards
- research caches
- abandoned facilities

### Research Archives

Research Archives are completed knowledge packages created from Research Notes and related materials.

Archives are installed into the Bloodline Keystone.

## Research Progression Loop

```text
Explore POIs
↓
Recover Research Notes
↓
Complete Research Project
↓
Create Research Archive
↓
Install Archive into Keystone
↓
Unlock Base Technology
```

## What Research Unlocks

Research can unlock:

- workstation upgrades
- base infrastructure
- crafting infrastructure
- advanced production
- defensive technology
- specialised laboratory systems

## What Research Does Not Unlock

Research does not directly evolve the player.

Player evolution belongs to DNA Helixes and Genetic Stability.
