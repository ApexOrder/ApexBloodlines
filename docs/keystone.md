# Bloodline Keystone

The Bloodline Keystone is the heart of base progression.

It is not a DNA storage device.

It is a recovered research archive and base technology system.

## Core Rule

> Bases evolve through knowledge.

The Keystone stores Research Archives, not DNA Helixes.

## What the Keystone Does

The Keystone controls base progression by storing completed Research Archives.

It can unlock:

- infrastructure
- workstations
- base upgrades
- technology branches
- production systems
- defensive systems

## What the Keystone Does Not Do

The Keystone does not give players personal power.

It does not store personal DNA Helixes.

It does not grant everyone in a group the same Bloodline abilities.

## Why This Matters

Separating player evolution from base progression protects multiplayer identity.

Each player evolves individually through DNA Helixes, while the group benefits from shared recovered knowledge through the Keystone.

## Design Rule

The player is the RPG.

The base is the technology tree.
