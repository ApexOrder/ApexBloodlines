## Welcome

Apex Bloodlines is a complete progression overhaul for 7 Days to Die.

Instead of becoming a master of everything through vanilla progression, players forge a Bloodline, earn Tomes, and advance their base through the Bloodline Keystone.

## Motto

**Forge Your Legacy. Build Your Bloodline.**
