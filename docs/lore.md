# Lore

## Project BLOODLINES

Before the collapse, Project BLOODLINES was a biotechnology initiative designed to help humanity survive increasingly hostile conditions.

Its purpose was not to create superheroes. It was to study controlled adaptation, resilience, cognition, recovery, endurance, and environmental survival.

When the outbreak began, the research was abandoned, scattered, damaged, and buried inside ruined laboratories, military facilities, field stations, and forgotten research outposts.

Survivors now rediscover fragments of that lost science.

## Core Theme

**Evolution begins with survival.**

The infected represent uncontrolled evolution. Bloodlines represent controlled evolution.

Players do not simply learn professions. They stabilise, sequence, and integrate genetic material to guide their survivor's development.

## Storytelling Rule

Bloodlines should not explain everything immediately.

Players should uncover the truth through:
- damaged DNA samples
- abandoned laboratories
- corrupted research notes
- old terminals
- specimen containers
- field reports
- environmental storytelling

The mystery is part of the progression.
